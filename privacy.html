Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how you can exercise your rights.

Effective date:

Who we are

"OKY Tech" ("we", "us") builds Shopify apps, high-performance websites, and automated testing systems. If you have questions about this policy, contact okytechsolutions@gmail.com.

Information we collect

Contact details

Name, email, company, and any message you send when you reach out or start a project.

Shopify merchant data

Store URL/ID, installed scopes, app settings, billing status, and data required for app functionality (e.g., metafields, orders metadata, theme/app extension settings) — accessed only with permissions you grant via Shopify OAuth.

Usage & diagnostics

Event logs, performance metrics, error traces, and basic device/session info to keep systems fast and stable.

We avoid collecting sensitive categories of data. If a feature requires additional data, we’ll explain why and request permission first.

How we use information

  • Provide, operate, and improve our apps, websites, and services.
  • Authenticate your store and maintain secure integrations with Shopify.
  • Deliver support, respond to inquiries, and send essential service updates.
  • Monitor reliability, enforce performance budgets, and prevent abuse.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce agreements.

Legal bases (EEA/UK)

  • Contract – to provide the services you request.
  • Legitimate interests – to keep our services secure, performant, and useful.
  • Consent – where required (e.g., certain analytics or marketing).
  • Legal obligation – when we must retain/process data to comply with the law.

Cookies & tracking

We may use strictly necessary cookies and similar technologies to keep sessions secure and remember preferences. If analytics are enabled, they are configured to be privacy-respectful and avoid storing personally identifying content whenever possible. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share data only with providers who help deliver our services, such as:

  • Shopify APIs and services when you use our apps.
  • Cloud hosting, storage, and delivery networks.
  • Monitoring, logging, and error reporting tools.
  • Payment processors for app billing (if applicable).

These providers are bound by agreements to protect your data and act only under our instructions.

Data retention

We keep data only as long as needed for the purposes outlined here, for the duration of an active account or project, and as required by law. When data is no longer necessary, we delete or anonymize it.

Security

  • OAuth best practices, least-privilege permissions, and signed webhooks.
  • Encrypted transport (HTTPS) and secure secrets management.
  • Automated tests and CI gates for performance and reliability.

No method is 100% secure, but we continuously improve defenses to reduce risk.

International transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your own. When we transfer data internationally, we use appropriate safeguards (for example, contractual clauses) as required by applicable law.

Your rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing, and request portability where applicable.
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent).

To exercise your rights, contact okytechsolutions@gmail.com. Shopify merchants can also manage app permissions from the Shopify admin.

Children's privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our services evolve. We will post any changes on this page and update the effective date.

Contact

Questions or requests? Email okytechsolutions@gmail.com.
